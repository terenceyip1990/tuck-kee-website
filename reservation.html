How to Find Us

By Car

From Ipoh town centre, head south on Jalan Dato Onn Jaafar then turn onto Jalan Yau Tet Shin. We're on the left. Limited street parking nearby.

Got Questions? · 問

Frequently Asked

  • Do you accept reservations?
    We don't accept advance reservations — Tuck Kee runs on a walk-in basis. Arrive and we'll seat you as a table opens. For groups of 8 or more, call 016-220 9361 so we can prepare for you.
  • What are your opening hours?
    Mon–Thu: 11:00 AM – 10:45 PM
    Fri–Sat: 11:00 AM – 11:30 PM
    Sunday: 10:00 AM – 10:45 PM

    Hours may vary on public holidays. Call 016-220 9361 to confirm.
  • Is there parking nearby?
    Street parking is available on Jalan Yau Tet Shin and surrounding streets. During peak hours (Fri/Sat evenings) parking can be limited. Arriving before 7 PM or taking Grab is smoothest.
  • Do you have indoor seating?
    We primarily offer outdoor seating in a traditional hawker-style setting — an authentic part of the Tuck Kee experience for over 60 years. Some covered seating is available nearby.
  • Do you cater for vegetarians?
    Yes — try our braised tofu, Ipoh bean sprouts, stir-fried kangkung and steamed rice. Tell our staff about dietary requirements when you order and we'll do our best to accommodate you.
  • Is Tuck Kee halal-certified?
    Tuck Kee is a traditional Chinese restaurant and is not halal-certified — some dishes contain pork. Guests with specific dietary requirements are encouraged to check with our staff; we are happy to advise on which dishes suit your needs.
